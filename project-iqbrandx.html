About the Project

IQ BrandX (أي كيو براندكس) is a Saudi digital agency based in the Eastern Region, specializing in comprehensive digital solutions for startups and SMEs. The agency needed a professional, high-converting corporate website that showcases their full range of services, attracts new clients, and establishes their brand authority in the competitive Saudi market.

The platform was built with Laravel on the backend — providing a full CMS, a partner marketplace module, a blog engine, and a built-in affiliate/marketer registration system. The frontend uses Bootstrap 5 with AOS animations, Swiper sliders, Glightbox media viewer and custom Arabic-first RTL styling.

Key highlights: live client portfolio (أعمالنا), a blog for digital marketing tips, an affiliate marketer program with shopping cart, and a dynamic partners showcase — fully bilingual and SEO-optimized.
