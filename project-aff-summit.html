Project Overview

The Affiliate & Influencers Summit Dubai is the premier event in the Middle East for the affiliate marketing and digital influence industry — bringing together the world's top affiliate marketers, social media influencers, Forex brokers, fintech companies, and digital advertising networks at the Ritz-Carlton Dubai International Financial Centre.

Organized by Smart Vision Group, this summit is the intersection of digital marketing and the financial trading world — speakers include C-level executives from global Forex brokers, performance marketing specialists, and influencer marketing leaders. The platform manages the full event lifecycle: public website, speaker profiles, sponsor listings, online registration, QR ticket generation, and real-time door scanning on event day.

Technical Challenges

Designing an 8-Tier Sponsorship System with Dynamic Benefits

Unlike standard event platforms with 3-4 sponsor tiers, AFF Summit required 8 distinct tiers — each with its own unique benefits (booth size, branding placements, speaking slots, social media mentions, website logo position, and more). Designing a flexible data model that lets the Smart Vision team manage all tiers and their benefits from the admin dashboard without code changes required careful schema design and a dynamic rendering system.

Managing Two Distinct Profiles — Speakers vs. Special Guests

This event uniquely separates participants into Speakers (who present sessions) and Special Guests (VIP attendees and celebrity influencers). Both required separate profile management interfaces in the admin, separate public website sections, and different data fields — speakers link to session topics while special guests link to their influence domain. Maintaining clean separation while using a unified management interface required careful admin UX design.

Premium UX Befitting a Ritz-Carlton Venue

The Ritz-Carlton DIFC hosts C-suite executives, high-net-worth individuals, and global brand directors. The public website needed to convey a premium, world-class feel commensurate with this caliber of venue and audience — requiring a polished design, high-quality imagery, smooth animations, and a conversion-optimized registration and sponsor application flow that matched the expectations of a sophisticated global audience.
