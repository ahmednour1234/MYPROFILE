Project Overview

Home Mark is a furniture wholesale shopping application developed by Syscom Technologies for the Lebanese market. The platform connects furniture buyers directly with wholesale suppliers — covering all categories of indoor and outdoor furniture in a single, easy-to-use mobile app.

Customers browse a rich product catalog organized by category, view detailed product specs and photos, and place wholesale orders easily. The admin dashboard gives the team full control over the catalog, orders, and customer database.

Technical Challenges

Large Catalog Performance

A wholesale catalog can contain hundreds of products with multiple high-res photos each. Delivering a fast mobile experience required efficient pagination, lazy image loading, and smart caching to stay responsive across large category listings.

Wholesale Quantity & Pricing Logic

Wholesale ordering involves minimum quantities, bulk pricing tiers, and stock availability. The system needed to accurately enforce wholesale rules — correct quantities, volume-based pricing, and real-time availability — without friction at checkout.

Bilingual RTL & LTR Layout

Supporting Arabic and English in Lebanon required full RTL/LTR layout implementation — ensuring every screen, card, and form renders correctly and naturally in both languages without layout breaks.
