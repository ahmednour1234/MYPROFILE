Project Overview

Future Nurseries Group (FNG) is a nursery management and parent communication platform developed by Syscom Technologies for the Lebanese early-education market. The platform bridges the gap between nursery classrooms and families — giving parents a secure, private window into their child's daily learning journey from drop-off to pick-up.

The ecosystem consists of a Flutter mobile app for parents and a full web-based admin dashboard for nursery staff. Parents browse classroom posts, read announcements, filter by grade or date, and receive push notifications — all through a clean, child-friendly interface designed for busy families.

Technical Challenges

Child-to-Parent Data Isolation

Multiple families share the same platform — each parent sees only their child's content while staff publish to entire grade groups. This required scoped content delivery with guardian-child relationship enforcement at every API endpoint.

Media-Heavy Feed Performance

Activity posts are media-rich — photos and video clips uploaded by staff. Delivering a smooth mobile feed required optimized compression, lazy loading, and efficient pagination to stay fast as the archive grows across the school year.

Real-Time Notification Reliability

Parents rely on notifications to stay informed. Ensuring reliable delivery across iOS and Android — through background restrictions, battery-saver settings, and varying network conditions — required robust FCM implementation with proper fallback handling.
