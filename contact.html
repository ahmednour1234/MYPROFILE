Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you take?

I specialize in backend-heavy projects: ERP systems, payment integrations, dashboards, CRMs, SaaS platforms, and enterprise automation. I work with businesses of all sizes across Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and the GCC.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A focused integration or dashboard can take 2–4 weeks. A full ERP system typically runs 3–6 months. I provide detailed timelines after understanding your requirements.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, fully remote. I have successfully delivered 40+ projects across 9 countries without on-site presence. Communication is handled via WhatsApp, email, and regular calls.

What technologies do you use?

Primarily Laravel (PHP), PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, Docker, Vue.js, and Python. I integrate with Stripe, URWAY, SAP, WhatsApp API, n8n, and any REST API your business needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. Most of my clients have ongoing maintenance and support agreements. I provide bug fixes, feature additions, performance tuning, and server monitoring post-launch.

Can you work with an existing codebase?

Absolutely. I regularly take over existing Laravel or PHP systems, audit the code, fix issues, and add new features. Just share the project details and I can assess it quickly.
