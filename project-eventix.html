Project Overview

Eventix is a cultural ticketing platform developed by Syscom Technologies for the Lebanese market — celebrating nightlife, comedy, and live performances. The platform operates under the mission 'Laugh and Dance for Earth', channeling ticket revenue toward funding real environmental projects through the arts.

The ecosystem includes a Flutter mobile app, an admin dashboard for events/tickets/revenue management, and an organizer portal for event creators to publish and track their events.

Mission

🌍

Laugh and Dance for Earth

Every ticket booked through Eventix contributes to funding real environmental projects — connecting entertainment with purpose, where artists and organizers who care about the planet create impact beyond the stage.

Key Features

✓
Event Discovery & Filtering Users browse events filtered by category, date, venue, city — with rich detail pages including lineup and capacity.
✓
Multi-Tier Ticket Booking Organizers set multiple ticket tiers — GA, VIP, Early Bird, Table — each with own price, quantity, and availability.
✓
QR Code Tickets After booking, QR tickets arrive in-app. Staff scan at the gate for seamless, paperless entry.
✓
Real-Time Sales Dashboard Live monitoring of ticket sales, remaining capacity, revenue by tier, and booking trends — updated in real time.
✓
Organizer Onboarding & Approval Organizers register and submit proposals for admin review — then manage their events independently through their portal.
✓
Push Notifications & Reminders Push notifications for new matching events, booking confirmations, event-day reminders, and last-seat alerts.

Technical Challenges

Ticket Inventory & Race Conditions

Concurrent last-ticket bookings required database-level locking and optimistic concurrency control to prevent overselling without slowing the experience.

Multi-Role Platform Architecture

Three user roles (users, organizers, admins) with clean RBAC boundaries — organizers see only their events while admins have full platform oversight.

Environmental Revenue Allocation

The platform automatically calculates, allocates, and reports the environmental contribution per event and organizer — maintaining transparent accounting for the cause.
